Kaiju No. Eight

Hibino Kafka has been forced to face Kaiju No. 8’s unstoppable power. As the Defense Force is shaken by his presence, a more dangerous threat than ever before approaches.

The first anime season of Kaiju No. 8, the mega-hit manga by Naoya Matsumoto with over 18 million copies in circulation, was released in April 2024.

The story became a worldwide hit as it followed Kafka chasing his dream to join the Defense Force, the bonds he forms with incredible characters, and the awe-inspiring battles against Kaiju.

Now, the second season the world has been waiting for arrives in 2025! Kafka is captured by the Defense Force and encounters the man known as their strongest warrior — Narumi Gen.
